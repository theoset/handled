About Handled

Connecting Gaborone's community, one task at a time.

Handled was built to solve a real problem: finding reliable, fairly-priced help in Gaborone is hard. We created a platform where every worker is identity-verified, every price is transparent, and every job is tracked from start to finish.

We're a Gaborone-native startup serving Block 3 to Tlokweng, Phakalane to Mokolodi — and growing every week. Whether you need a plumber at 8am or a cleaner on Saturday morning, Handled has you covered.
